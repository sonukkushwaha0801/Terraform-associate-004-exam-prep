# Booking Decision Matrix

## Do Not Book Yet

If any of the following are true:

* Mock exam scores below target
* External practice scores below 80%
* Multiple weak areas remain
* Confidence score below 3/5

Recommended Action:

```text
Continue studying and retake practice exams.
```

---

## Almost Ready

If:

* Mock exam scores meet target
* External practice scores are 75-80%
* Minor weak areas remain

Recommended Action:

```text
Revise weak areas and reassess in 3-5 days.
```

---

## Ready to Book

If:

* Mock exams consistently score 48+/57
* External practice exams consistently score 80%+
* No recurring weak areas
* Confidence score 4/5 or higher

Recommended Action:

```text
Schedule the exam.
```

# Remember

Do not aim for a perfect score.

Aim for:

* Consistent mock exam performance
* Strong understanding of core concepts
* Ability to identify common exam traps
* Confidence across all certification objectives

Passing the Terraform Associate (004) exam is the result of consistency, not memorization.
