# Final Readiness Assessment

## Not Ready

* Less than 70% on practice exams
* Unable to explain core concepts
* Weak understanding of state management

## Nearly Ready

* Consistently scoring 70–80%
* Minor knowledge gaps remain

## Exam Ready

* Consistently scoring 80–90%
* Comfortable with all objectives
* Completed multiple mock exams

## Strong Pass Likely

* Consistently scoring 90%+
* Able to explain concepts without notes
* Comfortable with scenario-based questions

---

# Recommendation

Schedule the Terraform Associate (004) exam only when:

* All domains are completed
* Flashcards are completed
* Revision sheets are completed
* Mock exams consistently score 48+/57
* External practice exams consistently score 80%+
* Exam traps are fully understood

Certification readiness is determined by consistency, not by a single high score.
