# Certification Readiness Status

## Green Zone

* Confidence Level: 4-5
* Consistent scores above target
* No recurring weak areas

Ready to schedule the exam.

---

## Yellow Zone

* Confidence Level: 3
* Some recurring weak areas
* Additional revision recommended

Delay scheduling until weak areas are resolved.

---

## Red Zone

* Confidence Level: 1-2
* Multiple recurring weak areas
* Inconsistent exam performance

Focus on revision before attempting the certification.

# Final Recommendation

The goal is not to achieve a perfect score.

The goal is to eliminate recurring mistakes and consistently demonstrate understanding across all Terraform Associate (004) exam objectives.
